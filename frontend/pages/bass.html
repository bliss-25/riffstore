How to Take Care of Your Bass Guitar

Clean Strings

Wipe bass strings after playing to keep their deep tone fresh.

Store Properly

Keep your bass in a gig bag or case away from humidity.

Check Tuning

Always tune your bass before playing for balanced sound.

Use a Stand

A bass stand protects the instrument from accidental falls.
