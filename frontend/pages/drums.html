How to Take Care of Your Drum Kit

Clean Drum Heads

Wipe drum heads regularly to remove dust and stick marks.

Store Properly

Keep drums in a dry room to protect wooden shells.

Tune Your Drums

Proper tuning improves the sound and balance of the kit.

Protect Cymbals

Use cymbal bags to prevent scratches and damage.
